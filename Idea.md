Core Product Concept
A single offline game containing a collection of diverse brain-training mini-games.
Mini-games vary by difficulty and brain usage (light → intensive).
New mini-games, maps, and content unlock as players progress.
Progression
Earn points by completing mini-games.
Level-based progression with increasing challenge.
Achievement badges and rank system.
Optional “Rebirth” mechanic to unlock harder content after completing the game.
Progress bars showing overall completion and progress within maps.
Motivation & Rewards
Cosmetic rewards unlocked through gameplay (not pay-to-win).
Multiple long-term goals: achievements, badges, collections, completion.
Sense of mastery through visible progression.
Gameplay
Variety of puzzle and logic mini-games (e.g., color vial puzzles).
Some mini-games include optional time/speedrun challenges.
Optional pressure mechanics (enemies, timers) where appropriate.
Procedural variation so repeated play feels different.
Learning
Integrate educational content naturally into gameplay.
Focus on mathematics and foreign languages.
Teach through gameplay rather than separate lessons.
Provide helpful feedback when players make mistakes without becoming frustrating.
Social Features
Offline-first gameplay.
Online synchronization when internet becomes available.
Global leaderboards for points and speedruns.
Support for 1–4 players where suitable.
Creativity
Allow children to create and share their own mini-games.
Introduce Vibe Coding as a creative extension of the game.
Design Principles
Respect players’ time—avoid manipulative retention mechanics or constant reminders.
Keep gameplay enjoyable, varied, and mentally stimulating.
Every session should contribute to visible, meaningful progress.
